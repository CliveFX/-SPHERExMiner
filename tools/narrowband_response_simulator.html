SPHEREx Narrowband Response Simulator

Bandpass-weighted spike footprint, not a delta-point search
Supplement-derived defaults: wavelength range `0.744-5.002 um`, six bands with resolving powers `R=39,41,41,35,112,128`, and `CBAND = CWAVE / R`. The response shape here is approximate until we ingest measured response curves.
The colored points are simulated SPHEREx measurements. A true narrowband source is expected to make a response-shaped local bump across overlapping samples, not necessarily a single isolated spike.
Local matched-filter template around the line. Nearby samples are weighted by their bandpass response at the line wavelength.
Detector/band plane: each cell is one simulated spectral sample. Bright cells are where the line leaks through the finite response.
